Our Story

Born from a Love of Good Food

Tasty Tedoz started as a small home kitchen operation with one simple belief: everyone deserves access to freshly made, genuinely delicious pastries — not the stale, mass-produced kind, but the type that reminds you of home.

What began as weekend baking sessions for family and friends quickly grew into something bigger as word spread about our flavours. We outgrew our home kitchen and moved into our current space in Porshe Estate Phase 2, where we serve our growing community every day.

Today we bake over 20 varieties of pastries and snacks, cater for events, and accept custom cake orders — all while staying true to our original promise: fresh, quality, and made with love.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver freshly baked, high-quality pastries and snacks that bring joy to every customer whether it's a casual snack, a birthday celebration, or an office event. We commit to consistency, quality, and warmth in everything we make.

Our Vision

To become the most-loved pastry brand in our community and beyond known for impeccable taste, creative flavours, and a customer-first culture. We aim to grow our product line and reach while never losing the soul of what makes us special: real ingredients and real care.

Core Values

The Principles We Bake By

These values guide every decision we make, from sourcing ingredients to serving our customers.

Freshness

We bake daily and never serve day-old products. Freshness is non-negotiable.

Quality

Only premium ingredients make it into our kitchen — no shortcuts, ever.

Integrity

Honest pricing, honest portions, and always delivering exactly what we promise.

Joy

We believe great food creates great memories. We bake with that in mind every single day.
